How to Sign In

Now that you have an account, it’s time to sign in.

1. Go to the Sign In Page

When you go to QLEAR and you’re not signed in, you will find the “Sign In” link on the top right corner of the page.

2. Sign In

Fill in your username and password, then click the “Sign In” button.

And you’re in!
